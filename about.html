Rector's Welcome

A Message from Our Leadership

Prof. Dilmurod K. Nabiyev

Doctor of Economics
Rector of Karshi State University

Dear Students,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to Karshi State University — a center of academic excellence, innovation, and national development.

With a deep-rooted commitment to education and over two decades of experience in the fields of science, higher education, and public service, I have had the honor of contributing to the advancement of Uzbekistan's academic and youth development landscape. From my early beginnings in the private sector to serving in various strategic roles — including Deputy State Advisor to the President and Minister of Physical Education and Sports — my journey has always been driven by a belief in the transformative power of knowledge.

Since February 2021, I have had the privilege of leading this esteemed institution. At Karshi State University, we strive to foster a culture of academic excellence, research-driven innovation, and social responsibility. Our goal is to prepare highly qualified professionals who will shape the future of our country and contribute meaningfully to the global community.

I invite each of you to be part of our vibrant academic family — to learn, to question, to create, and to lead. With a dedicated faculty, a dynamic student body, and a clear vision for the future, we are confident that Karshi State University will continue to be a beacon of knowledge and progress.

Thank you for choosing to walk this path with us.

Warm regards
